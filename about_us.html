About Us

    Welcome to Food, where culinary artistry meets exceptional service. Nestled in the heart of Egra, our restaurant is a haven for food enthusiasts seeking a memorable dining experience. Our journey began with a simple yet profound passion for bringing people together through the love of food.Founded in 2012, Food has grown from a humble idea into a beloved dining destination. Our founder, A.K Das, envisioned a place where guests could enjoy meticulously crafted dishes made from the freshest ingredients. Over the years, we have stayed true to this vision, ensuring every meal served is a testament to our dedication and love for the culinary arts.At Food, we believe that great food has the power to bring people together. We invite you to join us at Food for a dining experience like no other. Whether you are a long-time resident or a visitor to Egra, we promise to delight your senses with our exquisite food, exceptional service, and warm hospitality. Come, be a part of our story and create lasting memories at Food. For reservations or inquiries, please contact us. We look forward to serving you soon!
